THE CONTRACT

Gameplay Focus

JFW: Just a freelance worker is an intense first-person sniper/stealth simulator set in a procedural/random gritty, fog-shrouded city. Your primary tool is your precision rifle and your unwavering focus. You're not a soldier; you're an executor.

  • ▸
    High-Stakes Contracts: Take on government-contracted covert sniper missions to eliminate high-value targets and their armed henchmen.
  • ▸
    Urban Battlefield: Master the rooftops and vantage points of a sprawling cityscape where visibility and distance are your greatest challenge.
  • ▸
    One Shot, One Opportunity: Experience the tension of a single-shot opportunity and close-range pistol combat. Alerting enemies while in-doors is equal to suicide.
  • ▸
    Adjustable Challenge: Customize your experience with in-game settings for audio, graphics, mouse sensitivity, and blood/gore.
